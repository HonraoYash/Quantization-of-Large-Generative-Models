### What is Rectified Flow?

Rectified Flow is a type of normalization technique used in neural networks, particularly in the context of generative models like Variational Autoencoders (VAEs). The main idea behind Rectified Flow is to apply a rectifier function to the data before passing it through a transformation layer, such as a normalizing flow or a neural network.
